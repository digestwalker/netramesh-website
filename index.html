The Challenge

Cyber resilience is mandatory. Paying a fortune for it shouldn't be.

Most security teams don't fail for lack of effort — they're boxed in by cost, complexity, and tool sprawl. These are the gaps NetraMesh was built to close.

01

The "top-rated" tax

Best-in-class security has been priced like a luxury — inflated per-seat, per-GB, per-everything licensing that locks you in and drains the budget you'd rather spend on actual defense.

02

Fragmented tools, real blind spots

Dozens of disconnected tools mean context gets lost between them — analysts swivel between consoles while threats slip through the seams.

03

Alert fatigue, thin teams

Too many alerts, too few analysts. Without correlation and automation, the signal that matters drowns in the noise.

04

Faster threats, tighter rules

Attackers move in minutes, while compliance — UU PDP, ISO 27001, sector mandates — demands provable control. Slow detection means real loss, both financial and legal.

NetraMesh closes this gap without ripping out what you have — we integrate and enrich the open-source SIEM you can already run, so lean teams get enterprise-grade outcomes: risk down, response in seconds, audits passed, and ransomware stopped before it spreads — at a cost that doesn't break you.

◎Detect

OSS Integrator & Enricher

Bring your own open-source SIEM — we integrate and enrich it: normalized data, curated detection content, and correlation that turns raw events into actionable detections at scale. The proven core, made enterprise-grade.

Why it matters: open-source SIEM is powerful but raw — enrichment and correlation are what make it detect like the branded leaders.

UEBA

User & entity behavior analytics that baselines normal activity and flags the anomalies that signal insider and account threats.

Why it matters: stolen credentials and insiders bypass static rules — behavior analytics catches what signatures miss.

NIDS

Network intrusion detection that inspects traffic in real time — catching exploits, lateral movement, and C2 the moment they cross the wire.

Why it matters: endpoints can be blind or bypassed — the network never lies about what's actually moving.

⊕Investigate

Threat Hunting

A purpose-built workspace to hunt proactively — query unified data, test hypotheses, and pivot across signals to find what alerts miss.

Why it matters: the most dangerous threats don't trigger alerts — you have to go looking for them.

Case Management

Track every investigation end to end — evidence, timelines, collaboration, and audit trails in one workspace.

Why it matters: investigations lost in inboxes never close — one workspace keeps evidence, timeline, and audit intact.

Threat Intelligence Integrator

Aggregate and normalize feeds — commercial, open-source, and community — then auto-enrich every alert, IOC, and case with the context that makes it actionable.

Why it matters: an IP or hash means nothing alone — threat intel turns raw indicators into who, what, and how dangerous.

AI-Powered Analysis & Investigation

An AI copilot that triages alerts, reconstructs the full attack story, summarizes incidents, and turns analyst intent into working detection logic.

Why it matters: talent is scarce and time is short — AI multiplies every analyst instead of replacing them.

AI Threat Hunter

An AI agent that hunts around the clock — proposes hypotheses, queries your unified data, and surfaces stealthy threats no alert ever fired on.

Why it matters: skilled hunters are rare and human hours are finite — an AI hunter never sleeps and never stops looking.

Sandboxing

Detonate suspicious files and URLs in isolated environments to expose malware behavior before it reaches production.

Why it matters: unknown files are tomorrow's breach — detonate them safely before they ever touch production.

⚡Respond

SOAR

Orchestrate and automate response playbooks across your stack — cut mean-time-to-respond from hours to seconds.

Why it matters: manual response can't keep pace with automated attacks — playbooks contain threats in seconds.

Incident Response

Guided IR workflows that move teams from alert to containment to recovery — with built-in playbooks and forensics.

Why it matters: in a breach, minutes cost millions — guided IR moves teams from alert to recovery without guesswork.

Playbooks & Runbooks

A curated, version-controlled library of incident playbooks and operational runbooks — codified best practice your team can launch, adapt, and reuse instantly.

Why it matters: response shouldn't depend on who's on shift — codified playbooks make every responder act like your best one.

◇Understand

CMDB

A living configuration management database that maps every asset, dependency, and relationship across your environment.

Why it matters: you can't protect what you can't see — a living asset map gives every alert its context.

Vulnerability Management

Continuously discover, prioritize, and remediate vulnerabilities by real-world risk — not just CVSS noise.

Why it matters: you can't patch everything — prioritize by real exploitability, not endless CVSS noise.

KPI Awareness

A single pane of glass for your security posture — live KPIs, alert volumes, MTTR, risk score, and analyst workflows, all in real time.

Why it matters: you can't improve what you don't measure — clear KPIs turn security from a black box into decisions.

AI-Powered Context Awareness

AI that fuses asset, identity, and threat context around every signal — so each alert arrives already explained: what it touches, who owns it, and why it matters.

Why it matters: context is what separates a real incident from noise — and what lets a small team respond like a big one.

Adversary Simulation

Safely emulate real-world attacks and TTPs to validate defenses, measure detection coverage, and expose gaps before adversaries do.

Why it matters: untested defenses are just assumptions — simulation turns "we should be covered" into proof.

⬡Operate / Platform

Multi-Tenant Capability

Run many isolated tenants from one platform — separate data, policies, and dashboards per client or business unit, with role-based access throughout.

Why it matters: one shared platform with hard tenant isolation is how you serve many organizations without standing up many stacks.

Regulation Compliance

Map controls, evidence, and reporting to the frameworks you answer to — UU PDP, ISO 27001, and sector mandates — with audit-ready trails built in.

Why it matters: compliance failures carry real fines and legal risk — provable control turns audits from a scramble into a report.

MSSP-Ready

Purpose-built for managed security providers — onboard clients fast, operate them side by side, and white-label the whole experience as your own.

Why it matters: MSSPs win on margin and speed — running every client from one mesh is what makes that scale possible.

How is NetraMesh priced?

Fairly and transparently — capability-based, never a per-seat or per-GB tax, and no feature paywall. NetraMesh isn't a free giveaway; the point is that it's genuinely affordable and honestly priced, instead of the inflated, gatekept licensing incumbents charge.

What keeps it sustainable?

Fair product pricing, plus optional services — managed operation, professional support and SLAs, custom engineering, and white-label or partnership arrangements. You're charged a fair, predictable price and never overcharged or locked in just to stay protected.

How is it deployed — cloud or on-prem?

Is it production-ready and mature?
