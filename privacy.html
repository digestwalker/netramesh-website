Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2026

NetraMesh Labs operates netramesh.com as a static marketing and branding website. Our approach to privacy is simple: we collect nothing about you.

What we collect

No personal data. This site has no backend, no database, no login, and no analytics or advertising trackers. We do not set cookies. We do not use third-party scripts or content delivery networks — every asset is served from our own domain.

Local storage

The only thing stored is a single language preference (English or Bahasa Indonesia), kept in your browser's localStorage on your device. It never leaves your browser and is not transmitted to us or anyone else. You can clear it any time via your browser settings.

The contact form

The "Request a Demo" form does not submit data to any server. When you click submit, your own email client opens with a pre-filled message addressed to us — you remain in full control and choose whether to send it. Any information you then email us is used solely to respond to your enquiry.

Server logs

Like any website, our hosting provider may keep standard technical access logs (e.g. IP address, timestamp, requested page) for security and reliability. These are managed by the host and are not used for tracking or profiling.

Your rights

Because we hold no personal data through this site, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. For questions, or to exercise rights under applicable law (including Indonesia's Personal Data Protection Law / UU PDP), contact us at hello@netramesh.com.

Security & responsible disclosure

To report a security issue, please see our security.txt or email security@netramesh.com.

Changes

We may update this policy as the site evolves; the "last updated" date above will reflect any changes.
